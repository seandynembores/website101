The Solar System

The Solar System consists of the Sun and all the objects that orbit it, including eight planets, their moons, dwarf planets, comets, and asteroids. The main planets in order are:

  • Mercury
  • Venus
  • Earth
  • Mars
  • Jupiter
  • Saturn
  • Uranus
  • Neptune

The Milky Way Galaxy

The Milky Way is the galaxy that contains our Solar System. It's a barred spiral galaxy with hundreds of billions of stars. Our solar system is located in one of its outer spiral arms, called the Orion Arm.

It appears as a milky band of light in the night sky because of the many stars packed so closely together.
